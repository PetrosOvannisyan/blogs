

        <h2>What is tennis?</h2>
        <p>Tennis is a dynamic sport played on a rectangular court with a net dividing the playing area.</p>

        <h2>The rules</h2>
        <p>Players use racquets to hit a ball back and forth over the net, aiming to score points by making it difficult for their opponent to return the ball. </p>
        <img src="t2.jpg" class="blog-image">


        <h2>Why tennis is popular?</h2>
        <p>Known for its fast-paced nature and physical demands, tennis requires agility, endurance, and mental resilience.</p>
